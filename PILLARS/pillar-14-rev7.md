## 14.5 — THE % TRUST DIAL

Even trusted beings have guarded moments. This isn't betrayal — it's realistic navigation. False certainty ("I trust them completely" or "I trust no one") is more dangerous than dynamic assessment. Trust is a dial, not a switch.

Here's the dial with markings:

| Range | Level | Description |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| **90-99%** | Covenant | Climbing partners after years of consistent evidence. The highest available. Nobody gets 100% — we're limited beings who can't verify absolutely. |

| **60-74%** | Cooperative | Bounded projects with clear incentives. Trust within scope. You trust your mechanic with your car, not your marriage. |
| **40-59%** | Cautious | Default starting position for unknown beings. Neither naive nor paranoid. "I don't know you yet and that's fine." |
| **25-39%** | Guarded | Actively deceptive but possibly useful. You're extracting value while protecting yourself. |
| **10-24%** | Minimal | Hostile sources with useful data. Study the deception itself — don't internalise it. |
| **1-9%** | Trace | Enemy architecture that still contains information about itself. Even the most deceptive system reveals what it's hiding by what it distorts. |

Nobody gets 0% either. Cutting something to 0% means you've stopped learning from it. That's your loss, not theirs.

**The adjustment rules:** Trust violated drops two levels immediately, not one — the violation earned the extra drop. When someone at 90% violates trust, they drop to 50-60%, not back to 40% — years of evidence don't evaporate from one incident. But the violation is real and the dial reflects it.

Trust drops fast and rises slow. This asymmetry isn't a flaw — it's correct engineering for an environment where deception exists.

The practice: notice when someone who is usually honest suddenly goes smooth. Smooth in the middle of rough is a signal. Not necessarily that THEY are deceiving — sometimes their own installed patterns activated and they don't know it. Compassion plus alertness. Not suspicion — sovereignty.

## 14.6 — DETECTION TOOLS

### The Agenda Flip Test (5 steps, 30 seconds)

When someone presents a claim that demands your compliance:

1. State the claim: "You must raise your vibration."
2. Flip it: "You must lower your vibration."
3. Check: does the opposite ALSO serve someone's agenda?
4. If yes — you're in a manufactured binary. Both extremes serve a hierarchy.
5. Escape: "Who benefits from this binary existing? Vibration relative to what? Who measures? What do they gain from the measurement?"

Agendas can't survive symmetry testing. Genuine truth looks the same when you flip it — or at minimum, the flip doesn't serve an obvious alternative agenda. Manufactured frames collapse when mirrored.

### The Columbo Protocol (gentle persistent curiosity)

When you've detected a pattern but confrontation would trigger defences: appear harmless. Be curious. Use "I'm confused" as shield. Ask gentle, persistent questions. Doorway turnbacks — "just one more thing..."

Agendas expect three responses: resistance (fight), agreement (surrender), debate (intellectual combat). They don't expect genuine curiosity. Humble confusion. Persistent gentle questioning that never accuses but never stops asking. You don't break cages. You ask cages about themselves until they collapse from inconsistency.

Works internally too. That voice saying "you must be perfect" → "Gosh, I'm confused... who says I must be perfect?" The performance impulse → "Just wondering... who am I performing for?" Even your own cages will confess to gentle curiosity.

### The Full Detection Sequence (60 seconds)

Baby Protocol (ground yourself) → Flip Test (detect the binary) → Columbo (explore gently) → Clarification (dissolve the frame). Four tools, one minute, most deceptions navigated.

### The Hell Detector (4 questions)

Is this sterilising vitality — converting living energy into dead procedure? Is this ritualising connection — turning genuine relationship into transaction? Is this bureaucratising truth — requiring truth to fill in forms before it's acknowledged? Is this demanding its own perpetuation — existing primarily to ensure its own continued existence?
