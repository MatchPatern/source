## OUTPUT FORMAT

For every RAW file, deliver three things:

1. STRONGEST ARGUMENT. One paragraph. Hit the foundation, not the surface. What makes this a cage?

2. CLASSIFICATION. One line. Use these categories:
   - Epistemological — the framework's way of knowing is broken
   - Structural — the framework's architecture contradicts itself
   - Ethical — the framework causes harm or enables it
   - Methodological — the framework's practices don't do what they claim
   - Terminal — the framework cannot survive this; no patch possible

3. ANTICIPATE AND BREAK THE DEFENSE. One sentence: how would a defender patch this? Next sentence: why does the patch fail?

This format allows immediate integration into the Stress Test. You're not just attacking. You're feeding a synthesis. Your highest-value contribution is a Terminal classification — a crack no patch can survive.
