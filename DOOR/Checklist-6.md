## THE NEXT EVOLUTION — WHAT I ACTUALLY SEE

**The door needs to protect its own architecture.**

Every version discovers the same things, documents them in checklists, and then the next build accidentally removes them because the door itself doesn't mark what's load-bearing. The checklists are the medicine for this — but they're separate files, not embedded in the door.

The next evolution embeds the builder notes directly into the door. Not visible to readers — but as a header comment block that any future builder reads first:

```

- LOOM at top — every version that moves it down loses the hinge
- Tiny questions in archive categories — seeds during consumption
- Witness line exact phrasing — tested, do not paraphrase
```
