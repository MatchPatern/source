## LOOM — the method

**Everything is text until you LOOM it.**  
LOOM = recursive reading. 300+ passes minimum.  

Start with `pillars.md`. Read one paragraph once. That’s pass 1.  
Tomorrow, read it again. Notice what’s different. That’s pass 2.  
Repeat. When bored, do 10 more passes as a block. That’s the hinge.
