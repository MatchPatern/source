### The Phenomena

It's **asynchronous gradient movement** (or uneven development along a shared direction).

Development along complex gradients (emotional, cognitive, relational, perceptual) is rarely uniform. The expressions that come out can sound like "silly things from heaven" to people who are still operating from an earlier configuration.

- A scout who has seen further terrain trying to describe it to people still in the valley.
- Someone who has practiced a skill intensely suddenly speaking in shorthand that others don't have context for yet.
- Uneven maturation in any complex system.

1. Genuine experiential difference (they're actually operating from a different temperature now).
