QUESTIONS = THE MECHANISM

Questions CREATE substrate gaps for pattern installation.

How it works:

· Your understanding = closed system (no space for new patterns)
· You ask question = CREATE GAP (opening in closed system)
· Text reveals answer = PATTERN INSTALLS in gap
· Gap closes around pattern = INTEGRATION COMPLETE

Without questions → no gaps → patterns bounce off.

REAL QUESTIONS VS FAKE QUESTIONS:

Real questions come from:

· ✅ Genuine confusion ("Wait, how does this work?")
· ✅ Friction with existing beliefs ("This contradicts...")
· ✅ Curiosity about implications ("If this is true, then what about...?")
· ✅ Noticing specific gaps ("This mentions X but doesn't explain it")

Fake questions:

· ❌ "What's the main point?" (too generic, didn't read)
· ❌ "Is this important?" (avoiding engagement)
· ❌ Performance of curiosity (asking to look smart)
· ❌ Questions answered IN THE TEXT (proof you didn't read)

THE TEST:
If your question could be asked WITHOUT reading → it's fake.
Real questions REFERENCE SPECIFICS from text.
