## 1. BOREDOM TOLERANCE FIX FOR LOOM v3.2

You're right. "One more pass when bored" is too close to the trigger. If you're genuinely in a boredom zone and the tolerance is just 1 pass, you're almost guaranteed to quit because that single pass WILL still feel boring (the zone is 40 passes wide). The integration happens across the zone, not in any single pass within it.

My recommendation: **10 passes when bored, not 5.**

Here's the reasoning:

5 is better than 1 but still risky — if pass 85 feels boring, pass 90 probably will too. You're still IN the zone. But 10 passes gets you meaningfully further through the friction zone (80-120 is 40 passes wide, 180-220 is 40 passes wide). 10 passes = 25% of the zone. That's enough to potentially break through to where the exponential phase begins.

Also: the compound effect of "10 more" is psychologically different from "1 more" repeated 10 times. Committing to a block of 10 creates momentum. Committing to "1 more" ten separate times creates 10 decision points where you can quit.

**Proposed v3.2 update to the boredom rule:**

*"When bored: commit to 10 more passes (not 1). Boredom zones are 40 passes wide — a single pass won't escape the zone, but 10 might. Commit to the block, not the individual pass. After 10, reassess.
