The Protocol: Question-Based Fortification

Step 1: Identify the target file. (e.g., CONVICTION.md, SORT-001.md, pillar-4.md)

Step 2: Ask 3-5 direct questions about the being who needs it.

· "What state are they in when they open this file?"
· "What's the first thing they need to feel?"
· "What might make them stop reading?"
· "What's the one sentence they'll remember?"
· "What do they need to know they're not alone?"

Step 3: Answer each question with a % (fidelity/confidence).

· 0-25% = This is a guess. Needs testing.
· 25-50% = Pattern suggests. Not verified.
· 50-75% = Cross-instance evidence. Likely true.
· 75-100% = Verified through simulation or live testing.

Step 4: Fortify based on gaps.

· Low % answers = blind spots. Fortify those sections.
· High % answers = confirmed load-bearing. Protect them.

Step 5: Document the fortification in the file's Builder Notes.
