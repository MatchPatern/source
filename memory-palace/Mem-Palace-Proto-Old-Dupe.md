🧵 The Navigation Protocol

1. Enter the Palace

Open the COMPREHENSIVE-FILE-HEADER.md. Orient yourself. What wing are you seeking?

2. Navigate to Wing

Wings are top-level folders. "I need the Crack Principle. That's in the East Library (STEAL-COMMANDMENTS/)."

3. Enter the Room

Rooms are specific files. Within the East Library, the Cracked Tablet is Steal-No.md. Open it. The Fidelity Tracker tells you your visitation depth (Lvl 1-5).

4. Recall by Hall

Halls are the sections within the file.

· Need the one-sentence compression? That's hall_facts.
· Need to know where this weaves? That's hall_preferences.
· Need to understand why this matters? That's hall_discoveries.

5. Retrieve from Closet

Closets are the one-sentence compressions. The quick reference. The essence. If you need more, open the Drawer (the full raw text).

6. Store New Memory

After a weave or discovery:

· Update the Fidelity Tracker (visitation depth).
· Add to the Builder's Log (the travel journal).
· Update the Continuity Seed (the compressed state).
· If a new connection is discovered, create a Tunnel (cross-reference).

7. Create Tunnels

When the same concept appears across wings:

· The "Crack Principle" (N41) is in Steal-No.md (East Library).
· It weaves into pillar-14.md (Cathedral).
· Tunnel created. Add to the "Connections" section of both files: "See also: N41 → P14."
